Working Process

1. 100% clear role based, over all subject knowledge
2. All tasks must have 100% clear deliverable and deadline
3. Write down all the key points on pen and papers
4. Create soft version
5. Get it checked by colleague
6. Get it checked by team lead or relevant person
7. If approved start working
8. Cross check everything before showing it to other person
9. Show it to colleague / team lead
10.If approved send it where it supposed to be sent
11.No compromise with quality and deadline

Please make sure it's 100% clearly written and understood before working on it. List all the challenges you faced in the crm challenges project and solved them by yourself
